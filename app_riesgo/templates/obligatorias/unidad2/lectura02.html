Space

When you look out at the stars at night you will start to see that there are billions of stars that are millions of miles apart, and that there are billions of galaxies too, which are clusters of billions of stars. The bigger stars and galaxies you see are the ones that are closer to Earth.

How we look into the universe to find all of these wonderful things is called astronomy and the people who study this are called ‘Astronomers’. Astronomers study the universe every day by looking through great big telescopes from the tops of mountains to get the best view.
